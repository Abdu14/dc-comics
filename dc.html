CELEBRATING FEMINITY SINCE 2019
VANINA is a social enterprise that spontaneously grew out of a common passion since childhood. It evolved to become a lifestyle label, presenting seasonal collections of haute couture, evening dresses, and ready to wear; all proudly handcrafted in Pakistan.
Beneath their light and playful glamor, the Vanina products hide a passionately engaged activism that aims to promote local community development through the valorization of craftsmanship and environmental awareness.
